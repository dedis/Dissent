Detailed Description

This Round builds upon the verifiable key distribution, the Neff's Shuffle primitive, and verifiable decryption to produce a single exchange verifiable re-encryption mixnet.

The round can be used to either exchange keys (1024, 160) or messages (2048, 2047).
